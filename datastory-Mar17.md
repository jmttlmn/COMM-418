## [See which jobs are most threatened by AI and who may be able to adapt](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/interactive/2026/jobs-most-affected-ai-automation/?itid=hp_Biz/Nat_p006_f001)
### Washington Post

This story explores a study that aimed to find out which jobs were most vulnerable to being replaced by AI while also considering how much those particular jobs could adapt. I really like the graph at the start and how it expanded. Additionally, I like how they allowed you to search for a specific job type and see where it landed. The other visualization is further down and explores how male versus female dominated work spaces will be impacted. I like this graph a lot, it very clearly shows that the most at-risk jobs are majority women. I would've liked to see another graph like this, but for a different demographic, maybe age or average salary of the position. Depending on what the study/dataset included, I felt like there could've been a much deeper analysis or at least a few extra visualizations.
